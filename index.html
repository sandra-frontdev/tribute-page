INVENTOR CAREER

Incredible creations of Nikola Tesla:


  • AC MOTOR-He invented the first alternating current (AC) motor and developed AC generation and transmission technology.
  • THE TESLA COIL- It is used to produce high-voltage, low-current, high frequency alternating-current electricity.
  • THE MAGNIFYING TRANSMITTER- It is a high power harmonic oscillator that Nikola Tesla intended for the wireless transmission of electrical energy
  • THE TESLA TURBINE-The Tesla turbine is a bladeless centripetal flow turbine patented by Nikola Tesla in 1913. It is referred to as a bladeless turbine
  • THE INDUCTION MOTOR-An induction motor or asynchronous motor is an AC electric motor
